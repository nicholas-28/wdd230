Coast Information

The California Coast is one of the most iconic and scenic coasts in the country. The coastal stretch north of San Francisco in Sonoma County is home to the picturesque sea villages of Bodega, Bodega Bay, Jenner, Timber Cove, The Sea Ranch, and Gualala. Remember that you can enjoy the coast year-round.
