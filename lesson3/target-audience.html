Audience

Our audience is people who strive to make the world a better place with their ideas. These are young IT startups of about 20-30 years old and experienced businessmen. People who already have their own business and those who are just starting their own business, but who want to find like-minded people and benefits from cooperation, and somehow influence the laws adopted by the government.

Personas

Persona 1: A young male IT specialist from Moscow, about 26 years old. Not married. He was educated in Moscow in the field of IT, then worked for two years in London at Google. Then he moved to Tbilisi to start his own startup with friends. He uses a macbook, he constantly uses the Internet, as it is related to his work, about 4-8 hours a day.

Persona 2: The man, about 45 years old, Georgian, has lived in Tbilisi all his life. He inherited vineyards from his father and made a medium-sized business on this. Produces wine for the domestic market. Higher education in agro-industrial sphere. Married. Two children. Uses his laptop to surf the Internet for about 2-3 hours a day.:

Scenarios

  • Where can I find investors for my startup?
  • With whom to cooperate from similar businesses to improve production?
  • How to distribute your products outside of Georgia and start importing to the nearest countries?
  • How to make laws more loyal to businessmen from other countries?
  • How can I start my business in Georgia without Georgian citizenship?
