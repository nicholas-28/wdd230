Purpose

Our purpose is to help businesses develop in Tbilisi, and to enable both the local population and the flow of foreign citizens arriving here to start their own business and contribute to the development of the region. Georgia is a very rich country, and there is expected to be a large economic growth in various sectors of the economy. If you are interested in joining us, you can register on our website.
